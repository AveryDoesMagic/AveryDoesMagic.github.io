Elden Ring Review

Final Score: 9.5 / 10

Overview

Elden Ring takes the core ideas from the Dark Souls series and expands them into an enormous open world filled with secrets, danger, and exploration.

The combat remains challenging and strategic, while the world constantly rewards curiosity.

Pros

  • Massive world to explore
  • Deep and satisfying combat
  • Excellent boss encounters

Cons

  • Very difficult for new players
  • Some repeated bosses

Verdict

Elden Ring is one of the most ambitious action RPGs ever created and stands as a landmark release for the genre.
